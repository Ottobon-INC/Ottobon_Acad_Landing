# Ottobon Academy Operations Guide

## Terminology Shift: From "Administrators" to "Ops Support"

To better reflect the automated and support-oriented nature of our platform management, we have transitioned away from the term **"Administrator"**. 

The internal team responsible for platform maintenance, tutor onboarding, and automation is now referred to as **Ops Support**.

### Why Ops Support?
- **Automation First**: Our platform is designed to be self-scaling. Ops Support focuses on maintaining the high-fidelity AI and automation systems rather than manual intervention.
- **Tutor Empowerment**: Ops Support exists to provide tutors with the intelligence and tools they need to monetize their knowledge effectively.
- **Student Success**: By ensuring the "Training Wheels" framework is operating correctly across all courses, Ops Support indirectly guarantees student mastery.

### Core Responsibilities
1. **SRI Maintenance**: Ensuring Stateless Request Isolation (SRI) is performing optimally for secure, personalized chatbot interactions.
2. **Knowledge Base Hydration**: Helping tutors verify and upload their knowledge to create accurate Digital Twins.
3. **Enterprise Onboarding**: Supporting corporate clients in transitioning their in-house training to the Ottobon framework.
4. **Platform Integrity**: Monitoring the "No-Hallucination" guardrails to maintain 100% trust in expert-verified content.
